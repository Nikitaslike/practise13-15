AI is an incredible instrument

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a super useful technology that is transforming various industries. It helps in automating tasks, improving decision-making, and enhancing user experiences. AI is used in applications such as virtual assistants, recommendation systems, and autonomous vehicles, making our lives more efficient and convenient.

But you should notice that AI isn't able to replace jobs such as programmers because AI has only a stereotypical view, while real people have fantastic imagination and analytical skills.
